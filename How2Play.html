How to play the game of HearthStone

How to start HearthStone

Firstly, Hearthstone is a turn-based card game that you play online against other players around the world. The first thing to know about the game is that it is a card collecting game. You have the choice of deciding what class you want to play then you can making a deck of 30 cards with it includes minions, spells, quests or heroes that can influence the deck that you decide to make.

Now getting into the gameplay. The player is first introduced to a board that fit up to 7 minions on each side. The blue crystals are called Mana and are used as a way to play your cards. They will increase by 1 every turn at the start of every turn until they reach a max of 10. The user will start 3 or 4 cards depending if they go first or second

Both players will start with 30 health points and will battle till one of the reaches 0 and one person concedes if not the fight will continue

How to improve

Similar to other collection games the way for someone to improve is to keep playing the game to expand your collection of cards and improve the decks that you are using to rise up the ranks to become one of the best players. There are certain things that people need to take into account when playing this game. An important thing that players need to know the meta that they are playing in. Meta, short for most "effective tactics available" in gaming terminology is a generally agreed upon strategy by the community. Said strategy is considered to be the most optimal way to win/ has the best performance at a specific task. Some people have defined meta as an acronym meaning “most effective tactics available. This means that this will genrally affect who the player base will play and what decks theh will play.
Another thing that you will need to know to play this game is to have a small sense of what does what. For exmaple, theere are certains cards that can do niche things and other cards that cripple you if you do something wrong.

Recognising paterns

Recognising paterns inside this game is somewhat useful with knowing how to play against and what you are playing against. After playing for a certain amount of time, you will see that there are ways to pin-point what type of deck your opponent is playing. When you learn what type of deck that they are playing it will change the way you play the game a ceetain way because you will be expecting certain cards to be played when against some classes.

Meta Waves

Another thing to learn about this game is what the meta is. This is something that can be annoying to some players becaue it will limit them to what they are allowed to use whe playing the game when they want to be more competitive when they are playing against other people. The thing about meta is that it is able to change in a matter of seconds during an next expansion (New update) because of the new cards that they can add. It can fully shift the meta with it being able to make decks that were considered a top 10 deck to one that can't even be viable in competitive play.
